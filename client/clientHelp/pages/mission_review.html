Reviewing the Missions(One Week)

There are times when you or your supervisor want to check to see if the metrics were completed. This page provides a 7 day listing of missions entered during that period.

Click on the calander icon in the upper-right corner and choose the start date for the 7-day period. After choosing, the list of days and their associated missions will display for your review.
